PCA for financial analysis


Introduction

Pricipal component analysis is a statistical technique for dimesnion reduction of a dataset. The aim is to find 'Principal Components' (combinations of the vairables in the dataset) and rank them according to importance.
This can be an incredibly helpful technique:
  • Most algorithims will run significnatly faster will fewer dimensions.
  • Dataset become simpler and therefore easier to interpret.
  • Plotting the data becomes more simple.

How it works
